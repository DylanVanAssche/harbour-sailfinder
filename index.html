Sailfinder

Sailfinder is an unofficial Tinder client for Sailfish OS. Sailfinder is released under GPLv3, the source can be found in our Github repository where you can also report bugs.

Features

  • Like, dislike and superlike people
  • View their pictures and bio without looking at their profile
  • Change your Tinder discovery, location and other settings
  • View your profile: pictures, about, ...
  • Update your bio and gender
  • Upload pictures from FB
  • View your matches
  • Report & unmatch matches
  • Send messages
  • Like messages
  • Send & receive GIFs
